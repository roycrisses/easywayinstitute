Korean Language Class

EPS-TOPIK focused Korean training with structured practice and mock tests.

Overview

This course is designed for students preparing for EPS-TOPIK and Korean language basics for study/work. You will build strong foundations in reading, listening, grammar, and vocabulary with regular practice sets, revision classes, and mock tests.

Category

Language

Level

Beginner to Intermediate

Duration

What you will learn

  • Hangul reading and writing
  • Essential grammar for EPS-TOPIK
  • High-frequency vocabulary and sentence patterns
  • Listening and reading practice with exam pattern
  • Mock tests and performance feedback

Course features

  • Experienced instructors
  • Mock tests and revision classes
  • Exam-oriented materials
  • Guidance and support for EPS-TOPIK preparation
